Recording

I began recording when I was in my sophomore year of high school. I worked very hard on a few covers of songs artists I’ve been inspired by have written. I took a trip to Utah to record with my voice teacher. I had such an incredible time and did not realize how much I would then be interested in music production and recording. I then looked into my own equipment to be able to create a possible home studio. I began to learn about sound levels, reverb, and layering audio. I would love to continue learning and growing my recording skills.

My Music

Music is clearly a big part of my life. I don’t post videos often, but I have found that these are the best ways to share what songs I’ve worked on. I plan on continuing to post my work here. As I record more as well, I will share these songs too.
